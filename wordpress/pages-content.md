1. WP Admin → Pages → Add New

3. Content: pega el texto de abajo

```
# About AIPickd

AIPickd is an independent review site covering AI tools and SaaS products. We publish honest comparisons, in-depth reviews, and practical buying guides for marketers, developers, small business owners, and creators who want to pick the right tools without the hype.

## Why AIPickd exists

The AI tool space moves fast. Every week there's a new "top 50 AI tools" roundup, and most of them are noise — lists ranked by affiliate commission rather than genuine usefulness. We started AIPickd because we were tired of that. We wanted a place where reviews are deep, comparisons are honest, and recommendations are specific to real use cases.

## How we review

Every product we cover goes through the same process:

1. **Hands-on testing.** We use the product for a meaningful workflow, not just a demo.
2. **Real pricing analysis.** We dig into the fine print — renewal rates, hidden fees, what's actually included at each tier.
3. **Honest pros AND cons.** No tool is perfect. If we don't mention limitations, we're not doing our job.
4. **Specific recommendations.** "Best AI writing tool" is meaningless without context. We tell you who should pick what, for what use case.

## Who we write for

- **Small business owners** evaluating AI tools to save time and money
- **Marketers and creators** building content operations
- **Developers** picking the right AI coding and infrastructure tools
- **Solopreneurs and freelancers** maximizing output with AI

## How we make money

AIPickd earns revenue through affiliate partnerships with some of the products we review. When you click a link and sign up for a service, we may receive a commission at no extra cost to you.

**This does not influence our reviews.** We recommend products based on quality, not commission rates. Several of the tools we praise highly (ChatGPT, Claude's free tier, open-source alternatives) pay us zero. Several tools that pay the highest commissions get critical reviews when they deserve them.

## Contact

Questions, feedback, or want to suggest a tool we should review? Email us at hello@aipickd.com.

Follow our weekly newsletter for the latest reviews and deals on AI tools.
```

```
# Affiliate Disclosure

**Last updated:** April 2026

## The Short Version

AIPickd makes money through affiliate partnerships. When you click links on our site and sign up for products, we may earn a commission — **at no additional cost to you**. This does not influence our reviews or recommendations.

## The Full Version

### What are affiliate links?

Affiliate links are special URLs that contain tracking codes. When you click one and then sign up for a service or buy a product, the company knows AIPickd referred you and pays us a commission. This is similar to a finder's fee.

### Who do we partner with?

AIPickd participates in several affiliate programs, including but not limited to:

- **Amazon Associates** — books, hardware, and other products on Amazon
- **Impact** — partnerships with Jasper, ClickUp, Semrush, and others
- **PartnerStack** — partnerships with Notion, Monday.com, Make.com, and others
- **Direct partnerships** with Hostinger, Copy.ai, Writesonic, and various SaaS companies

### Does this affect our reviews?

No. Here's how we keep editorial integrity:

1. **We review products, not commissions.** Our ranking methodology is based on product quality, fit for specific use cases, and real-world testing — not on which company pays the most.
2. **We recommend products that pay us zero.** Our guides often highlight free tools, open-source alternatives, and products without affiliate programs when they're the right fit.
3. **We publish honest negatives.** If a product that pays high commissions has real problems, we say so. If a product that pays us nothing is genuinely better, we say that too.

### How to tell if a link is an affiliate link

All affiliate links on AIPickd are nofollow and include tracking parameters. You can hover over any link to see its destination in your browser. We also add small disclosure notes next to prominent affiliate links where appropriate.

### Your costs

Clicking an affiliate link and signing up does NOT cost you more. The commission comes from the company's marketing budget, not from your pocket. In many cases, using our affiliate links actually provides you with exclusive discounts or extended trials.

### FTC compliance

This disclosure is provided in compliance with 16 CFR § 255, the Federal Trade Commission's Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising.

## Questions?

If you have questions about our affiliate relationships or editorial policies, email us at hello@aipickd.com.
```

## ✅ Checklist rápido de creación

Una vez dentro de WP Admin → Pages → Add New, crea estas 5 páginas:

- [ ] About
- [ ] Privacy Policy
- [ ] Terms of Service
- [ ] Affiliate Disclosure
- [ ] Contact

Cada una: pega el título y el contenido de arriba → Publish. Listo manito.

## 🔗 Después de publicar las 5 páginas

Ve a WP Admin → **Appearance → Menus**:
1. Create a new menu: "Footer"
2. Añade las 5 páginas al menú
3. Select Display Location: "Footer Menu" (o similar según el tema)
4. Save

Con eso Google sabe que eres un sitio legítimo y bien estructurado → mejor SEO.
